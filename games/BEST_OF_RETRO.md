# The Ultimate Retro Collection: RG40XX V Edition
*Created on Friday, March 13, 2026*

This guide curates your collection for the Anbernic RG40XX V, focusing on the 4:3 aspect ratio and the H700 chipset's strengths.

---

## 🏆 Franchise Masterpieces (The "If You Only Play One" List)

When time is limited, these are the definitive entries in each major series.

### 🗡️ The Metroidvania Group
*   **Selected:** *Castlevania: Symphony of the Night* (PS1)
*   **Why:** While *Super Metroid* is perfect, *SOTN* defined the "Vania" half of the genre with its RPG systems, incredible soundtrack, and "Inverted Castle" twist. It is the peak of 2D PS1 artistry.
*   **Others to Move to "Backlog":** *Super Metroid* (SNES), *Castlevania: Aria of Sorrow* (GBA).

### 👊 The Street Fighter Group
*   **Selected:** *Street Fighter Alpha 3* (Arcade - `sfa3u`)
*   **Why:** It has the largest roster and the "ISM" system which allows you to play in the style of SF2, Alpha, or X. It looks stunning on the RG40XX V's screen.
*   **Others to Move to "Backlog":** *SF2: World Warrior*, *SSF2 Turbo*.

### ⚔️ The 16-Bit RPG Group
*   **Selected:** *Chrono Trigger* (SNES)
*   **Why:** It is the "perfect" RPG. No grinding required, multiple endings, and a brisk 20-hour pace that respects your time more than the 60-hour *Final Fantasy* epics.
*   **Others to Move to "Backlog":** *Final Fantasy VI*, *Breath of Fire II*.

### 🦔 The Sonic Group
*   **Selected:** *Sonic 3 & Knuckles* (Genesis - Combined Rom)
*   **Why:** This is the complete vision of Sonic 3. It features save slots, three playable characters, and the best level design in the series.
*   **Others to Move to "Backlog":** *Sonic 2*, *Sonic CD*.

### 🔫 The Metal Slug Group
*   **Selected:** *Metal Slug X* (Arcade - `mslugx`)
*   **Why:** It is a remixed version of *Metal Slug 2* that fixes the slowdown issues and adds more weapons and "weirdness." It's more balanced than *Metal Slug 3*.
*   **Others to Move to "Backlog":** *Metal Slug 1*, *Metal Slug 3*.
